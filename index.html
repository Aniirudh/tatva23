About TATVA

TATVA, the techno-cultural festival of BNM Institute of Technology organised by the student community of BNMIT. TATVA is an eagerly awaited inter-collegiate fest all across Bengaluru. TATVA attracts the best technical talent to compete with their peers, prove their competence and represent their colleges in a competitive and conquering spirit.

Each of the events of TATVA is designed to provide a unique challenge and invoke a different thought process and out-of-the-box thinking. TATVA brings innovation and freshness in its event by introducing at least one new event every year to bring in new challenges among the participants. TATVA 2023 is expected to host more than 5000 students between the ages of 18 and 22 years.

About LAKSHYA

As we incorporate the 17 goals towards a sustainable future, we join the cause to lead a universal call of action to protect the planet, and ensure that all people enjoy peace and prosperity. With this theme, TATVA aims to raise awareness among students about the importance of sustainability and inspire them to take action towards building a more equitable and sustainable world for everyone.
